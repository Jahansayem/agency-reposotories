### 9.1 Risk: Loss Ratio Spike

**Scenario:** Major claim pushes LR from 68% to 78%

| Impact | Value |

| Q1-Q3 Advances | Lost (if >71%) |

**Mitigation:**
1. Review high-risk policies quarterly
2. Avoid known bad risks (new drivers, claims history)
3. Consider non-renewing persistently unprofitable accounts
4. Monitor 12MM and 24MM LR monthly

### 9.2 Risk: Book Sale Mid-Year

**Scenario:** Derrick sells satellite or portion of book

| Impact | Consequence |
